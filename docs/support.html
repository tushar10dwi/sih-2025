Account & Login

How do I create an account?

Click "Sign Up" on the homepage, enter your details, and verify your email.

I forgot my password, what do I do?

Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page to reset via email.

Can I use Google or Facebook login?

Yes, you can sign in instantly using your Google or Facebook account.

Career Roadmap & Preferences

How is my personalized roadmap created?

Our AI analyzes your profile, aptitude quiz, and preferences to suggest exams, courses, and colleges.

Can I update my preferences later?

Yes, you can change your career interests anytime from the Preferences & Settings page.

How accurate are the recommendations?

Recommendations are based on verified data and your inputs, but final decisions should also include mentor/parent guidance.

Notifications & Deadlines

What kind of notifications will I receive?

You'll get alerts for exam registrations, college application deadlines, scholarships, and roadmap milestones.

Can I customize notifications?

Yes, you can enable/disable email, SMS, and push notifications in Preferences & Settings.

Do reminders sync across devices?

Yes, as long as you log in with the same account, your reminders follow you everywhere.

Privacy & Security

How is my data protected?

All personal data is encrypted and securely stored. We never sell or share your information.

Can I delete my account permanently?

Yes, you can request account deletion from Preferences & Settings under Privacy.

Do you comply with data protection laws?

Yes, our system follows GDPR and local data protection guidelines.

General Help

Is this platform free to use?

Yes, this platform is 100% free to use.

Who can I contact for urgent help?

Use the Contact Support form below or reach us at support@navidisha.com.

Do you have offline functionality?

Yes, our PWA allows limited offline use. Data syncs when you reconnect.
